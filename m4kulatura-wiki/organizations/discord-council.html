Weapons

  • SCI-2: The so-called "Standard Council Issue" is a assault rifle manufactured in Australia. It has 3 firing modes: full-auto, 5.56 round mode and a special grenade launcher mode. Two magazines are packed with .300 AAC rounds and 5.56 rounds used for sniper mode.

  • SOCI-420: This is the experimental energy weapon that is used by the most valued soldiers working for the discord council, it is multi-purpose and has the size of a handgun. It requires a lot of experience to use it properly, since the cartridges are expensive as fuck and the weapon itself is hard to handle.
