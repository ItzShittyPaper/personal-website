The Map

The map of m4kulatura's server is a city based on Radzionków. near to the coast is a street for rich people, this district consists of around 8 houses. Behind it there is a main residential area made of a few "commie blocks". Next to it there is a small village nearby, a ghetto and the city center.

The center is the place for "politics". Inside of it there is the parliament and the president's house. The ghetto is for anime girls.

The Story

Prologue

The roleplay story is set in the 1980s and the present day, depending on the point of view.

A far-right wing political party won the election. They started bullying chinese people and "anime girls" right away from the start. Most of the chinese population was locked down in a ghetto - a locked down part of the town. One day a typical anime boy (he doesn't have a name, but he is played by m4kulatura so i will call him that) went to his friend after class. Because of the restrictions imposed by their leaders - they couldn't meet at night - but they disregarded that and met anyway. A few hours later a SS officer was patrolling and they decided to escape in the night. After a few days they were caught and returned to the ghetto. Later they told the story to the schools principal. A few hours later everybody was killed except m4kulatura.
