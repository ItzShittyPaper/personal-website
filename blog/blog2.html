Making a game

Welcome to my next (very serious ;D) blog. Today i will talk about making my game in godot.

The current state

First i want to address the current state of my project, cuz it's kinda important. Most of the mechanics are complete (even if plagued with bugs). You can shoot people, you can reload, you can switch weapons etc.

My main goal at the time of writing this is fixing the bugs and publishing a demo with i don't know, thirty minutes of gameplay. Next i will work on the campaign and custom modes.

What's actually in the game?

Currently there is:

  • Bunnyhopping mechanics
  • Jump-pads
  • Anti-gravity pads / areas
  • Enemies with AI
  • Health system (+ healing stations simillar to those in Half-Life)
  • 5 weapons (sadly only 3 work, the SPAS-12 and melee attacks are broken)
  • Reloading (you always have ammo, click R to replenish your mag)
  • Crouching / Sneaking
  • Scope feature
